Frequently Asked Questions

What is RTI?
RTI stands for Right to Information, a fundamental right that empowers citizens to seek information from public authorities.
How can I file an RTI application online?
1- Visit our portal and register/login.
2- Select the appropriate department, provide details, and draft your RTI application.
3- Pay the prescribed fee online.
What information can I request through RTI?
You can seek information related to government activities, policies, decisions, and more.
How long does it take to get a response?
The authority is required to respond within 30 days, but you can check the status on our portal.
What if I need assistance in filing an RTI application?
We provide step-by-step guidance on our portal. Additionally, you can contact our support team for assistance.
Can I file an RTI application for any public authority?
Yes, you can file an RTI application for information held by any public authority under the government.
Is the RTI process the same for all states in India?
The process is standardized, but slight variations may exist. Ensure you select the correct jurisdiction while filing.
Is there a fee for filing an RTI application?
Yes, a nominal fee is applicable, which can be paid online through our secure payment gateway.

For any further assistance, please contact our support team or refer to the user guide available on the portal.
