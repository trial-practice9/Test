WHAT IS RTI ?

RTI stands for "Right to Information." It is a legal right that allows citizens to access information held by public authorities. The primary goal of RTI is to promote transparency and accountability in government organizations. Individuals can file RTI requests to obtain information on government actions, decisions, policies, and other relevant matters. This right is essential for fostering a more open and democratic society by empowering citizens with access to information about the functioning of public institutions.
